Charities in the Philippines

ABS-CBN Lingkod Kapamilya

ABS-CBN Foundation is committed to making a positive impact in the lives of Filipinos, especially the underserved. We do this through our advocacies on children’s rights and development, education, the environment, disaster resilience, and sustainable livelihood. Your support serves as the cornerstone of our relentless pursuit of hope, love, and compassion.

Save the Children Philippines

Save the Children, the world’s leading independent children’s organization, has been working in the Philippines for over four decades and is dedicated to transforming children’s lives and the future we share.

World Vision PH

Our focus is on helping the most vulnerable children overcome poverty and experience fullness of life. We help children of all backgrounds, even in the most dangerous places, inspired by our Christian faith. As the largest non-profit organization in the Philippines with over 65 years of experience, World Vision helps over 1 million children every year, with the support of thousands of Filipino sponsors and donors.
